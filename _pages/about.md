I am a Master’s student in Computer Engineering at New York University (NYU), where I also conduct research under the guidance of [Dr. Samrachana Adhikari](https://samrachana.com). 

Previously, I was a research intern at the [Fujii Lab](https://www.fujii.nuee.nagoya-u.ac.jp/index-e.html), Nagoya University, mentored by [Dr. Fujii Toshiaki](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=CcGm4YQAAAAJ&hl=en). I was selected as one of 13 graduate students from the U.S. and Canada for the prestigious JUACEP Summer Research Fellowship - a competitive, 10-week research program funded by the Government of Japan.

I earned my Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science and Engineering with Honors in Artificial Intelligence. During my undergrad, I was fortunate to work on exciting research under [Dr. Palash Ghosal](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=S1OTcXoAAAAJ&hl=en). 

I am broadly interested in the science behind deep learning and reinforcement learning, especially in the realm of computer vision. Most recently, I have been interested in developing and understanding models under image compression.
